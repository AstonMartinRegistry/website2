Summer 2014 - The idea of a mid-engined Formula 1 car is brought to the attention of Andy Palmer

December 29, 2014 - Adrian draws a first sketch of the Valkyrie layout

February 2015 - Swiss dealers agree to finance a few cars

April 2015 - Adrian designs the car

July 13, 2015 - First presentation of the project between all parties, financing secured

August 10, 2015 - Andrian completes his wish list for the car

Winter 2015 - Antonio Lo Re's design proposal for Valkyrie is chosen

March 15, 2016 - Nicknamed AM-RB-001

March 17, 2016 - Project is a go, contract between AM and RB are signed

July 5, 2016 - First full-size model of AM-RB-001 is presented to the public

May 26, 2017 - Valkyrie is shown to VIP during the Monaco GP, it is 90% complete
